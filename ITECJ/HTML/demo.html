  • This special one-day event salutes teachers as exemplary role models who have and continue to educate, inspire, and lead the UAE and its people on their journey of development and achievement.

    Organized jointly by the Ministry of Education and the Abu Dhabi Education Council, the Qudwa Teachers’ Forum 2016 is being held under the patronage of His Highness Sheikh Mohamed bin Zayed Al Nahyan, Crown Prince of Abu Dhabi, Deputy Supreme Commander of the UAE Armed Forces, and Chairman of the Executive Council of the Emirate of Abu Dhabi.
